Best Ayurvedic Cures To Get Rid of Kidney Stones

Kidney stones are a common condition that affects Millions of People in the World. There are many ways to help prevent Kidney Stones or get rid of them, but Ayurveda is one of the best ways to do so. Read on to know different Ayurvedic Cures that can help you get rid of your Kidney Stones.

What is Kidney Stone?

Kidney Stones are small, hard deposits that form in the Kidney. They are made up of substances that are normally found in the Urine, such as Calcium, Oxalate, and Phosphate. When these substances become concentrated, they can form Crystals. Over time, these Crystals can grow larger and become kidney Stones.Kidney Stones can cause a lot of pain. They can also block the flow of Urine and lead to other problems, such as Urinary Tract Infections. Fortunately, there are a number of Ayurvedic Cures that can help to get rid of Kidney Stones.

Symptoms

There are a number of symptoms that can indicate the presence of Kidney Stones. These include pain in the Lower Back or Abdomen, Blood in the Urine, Nausea, and Vomiting, and Fever. If you experience any of these symptoms, it is important to see a doctor as soon as possible so that the Kidney stones can be Diagnosed and Treated.

Causes

There are several different things that can cause Kidney Stones. One of the most common causes is Dehydration. When your body doesn't have enough Fluids, the Minerals in your Urine can start to Crystallize and form Stones. Other causes include eating a Diet high in Salt or Animal Protein, having a family history of Kidney Stones, or having certain Medical Conditions such as Gout or Crohn's Disease.

Ayurvedic Approach to cure Kidney

Ayurvedic Cures are a popular treatment option for Kidney Stones. There are a number of different Herbs and Spices that can be used to help dissolve Kidney Stones and alleviate symptoms. Following are some of the natural Remedies to cure Kidney Stone:

1. Drink plenty of fluids.

Drinking Eight to Ten Glasses of Water a day will help flush out your system and prevent Kidney Stones from forming.

2. Eat a Healthy Diet.

A Diet rich in Fruits and Vegetables helps to keep your Kidneys healthy and free from Stones.

3. Avoid certain foods.

Some foods, such as Chocolate and Caffeine, can increase your risk of developing Kidney Stones.

Ayurvedic Cures for Kidney Stones

There are a number of different ayurvedic cures for Kidney Stones.

• One Popular Cure is to drink a Mixture of Lemon Juice and Olive Oil. This Mixture helps to break down the Stones and flush them out of the body.

• Another popular Cure is to drink a Mixture of Apple Cider Vinegar and Water. This Mixture also helps to break down the Stones and flush them out of the body.

• A Third popular Cure is to drink a Mixture of Baking Soda and Water. This Mixture helps to Neutralize the Acids in the body that contribute to Kidney Stones.

Overall, there are a number of different Ayurvedic Cures for kidney Stones. These Cures can help to break down the Stones and flush them out of the body.
