About Us

Founder

Shiv Kumar Goyal is initially a non-medical science student till higher secondary then graduated in Commerce from Punjab University Chandigarh, then passed CA inter from Institute of Chartered Accountants of India New Delhi. He is also a graduate in Law. He is a post-graduate in Yoga from Uttarakhand Open University. He finally diverted to Social Field and has been running Ayushyam since May 2011.

Mission & Vision

Ayushyam’s vision is to establish the credibility of the Indian System of medicine & other non-medicine therapies. Our vision is to make health service within the approach of common men. Research & Development of Vedic Medical System is an integral part of our organisation.

Ayushyam Wellness
